Investment Strategy:

  • Target emerging neighborhoods with irreplaceable historic buildings.
  • Initial focus on Corktown, Southwest, Jefferson Chalmers, and West Village.
  • Deliver high-quality mixed-use buildings with an emphasis on residential.
  • Drive value by connecting underutilized, character-rich assets with the established urban fabric.
  • Work with local communities to create contextually-appropriate products

Target Criteria:

  • Project Size: Above the capacity of casual investors and below the interest of institutional players (~$1M-20M).
  • Asset Type: Preference for existing structures with interim income and value-add or redevelopment potential.
  • Product: Well-built, distinctive, historic buildings with suitable dimensions for modernized residential conversion.
  • Location: Infill locations in the path of sustained growth. Potential drivers:emographic trends, policy changes,
    infrastructure investments, evolving employment opportunities.
